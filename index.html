Personalised Muhūrtha Advisor

Evaluates 26 Vedic rules at every 30-minute window and finds auspicious muhūrthas tailored to your natal chart.

How to use:

  1. Select Event: Choose what you're planning (marriage, journey, business, etc.)
  2. Enter Birth Details: Your date, time & location (or just your Nakshatra)
  3. Set Search Period: Date range to search for auspicious dates
  4. Configure Filters: Choose quality level and optimization options
  5. Click Search: Find the best dates and review results

Filter Guide:

  • Prashna Flaws: Exclude dates with horary astrology defects
  • Ekavimshati Doshas: Exclude dates with 21 great evils
  • Hide "Avoid" Yogas: Exclude dates tagged as unfavorable by the engine
  • Auto-Optimize Pañcaka: Automatically shift times to avoid bad Pañcaka (5-day cycles)

Results: Each date shows score, flaws, blessings, and which Vedic rules it satisfies.
